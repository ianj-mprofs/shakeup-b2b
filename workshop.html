WORKSHOP OVERVIEW

Your day-to-day marketing gig doesn't make you a writer. Or does it?

Everybody writes. Especially marketers. Nurture emails, social updates, SEO, stakeholder presentations (and an endless list more)… they all count as writing. Each requires you to communicate, captivate, and convince your audience.

Whether you're a new writer connecting with customers or a non-writer seeking big-ups from the higher-ups, this LIVE workshop will help you go from cautious to confident.

Marketing Writing for New Writers and Non-Writers focuses on audience-centric writing rather than the nuts and bolts of product-centric features-advantages-benefits content. You'll learn…

  • How to adopt a brand voice for your writing
  • How to adjust your tone for different contexts
  • How to write compelling headlines
  • How to hone your pathological empathy so you can create content that resonates with your audience

WORKSHOP OVERVIEW

Demand generation and marketing automation are two powerful elements of a smart marketer's plan for B2B success. And the good news is, getting the best results from them doesn't require magic—just good science!

Instructor Kenda Macdonald is here to get you started in Attract and Nurture: Demand Generation. She'll provide the low-down on using behavioral psychology-based evidence to trigger your desired actions. You'll learn how to toss out your typical funnel and instead master life cycle marketing, the buyer purchase formula, and the five buyer awareness stages.

This is a LIVE workshop only and will not be available on-demand. Sorry, we do not provide workshop slides as a handout, but there are plenty of other online resources, tools, templates, and takeaway materials.

Attract and Nurture: Demand Generation focuses on the first two phases of life cycle marketing—attract and nurture. You'll learn…

  • How to combine automation, buyer psychology, and content for demand generation success
  • How to identify and use the right type of content to attract and nurture prospects
  • How and why lead magnets work for you (and not just during the attract phase!)
  • Three types of nurture campaigns
